INSTALLTION
  Double click install.cmd to register the shell extensions.
  Use your favorite text editor to open config.xml and have fun.

UNINSTALLTION
  Double click uninstall.cmd to unregister the shell extensions, and then delete the files.
  KuShellExtension doesn't put any additional files in your disk.

DOCUMENTATION
  Read the comments in config.xml, or read it online at
  http://sourceforge.net/apps/mediawiki/kushellext/index.php?title=Configuration
